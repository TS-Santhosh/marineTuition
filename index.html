About Me

Hi, I'm Santhosh TS, a marine professional with 42 years of experience, including 32 years as a Chief Engineer on various vessels. Many of you may know me from my time as Visiting Faculty at IMEI, Eurotech, or CUSAT, where I conducted classes for Class 1 & 2 Engineers, Second Mates, Phase 2, ASM, revalidation courses, simulator training, oil tanker and chemical tanker safety courses, firefighting, and other safety courses.

My Services

I am now offering online classes for junior engineers preparing for the Class 4 Marine Engineers Exam. These sessions are tailored to address the gaps in traditional learning resources, focusing on practical troubleshooting, modern machinery, and MMD exam requirements. My goal is to empower students with the skills and knowledge needed for both exams and onboard challenges.

I kindly request your support in recommending these classes to junior engineers in your network or onboard. Your endorsement would make a significant difference.
